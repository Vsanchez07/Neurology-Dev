Michelle Fernandez, APRN

Nurse Practitioner

Michelle Salcedo Fernandez completed her Master’s of Science in Nursing for Family Nurse Practitioner studies from Post University (Connecticut) in 2021. She was born and raised in the Philippines and finished two bachelors namely Medical Technology and Nursing. Michelle has over 14 years of clinical experience as a nurse prior to pursing her NP career. Her friends and family described her as “go getter” and very ambitious regardless of how loaded her plate is. She is a wife, a mom of 3 toddlers and worked two jobs while doing her NP program. Her determination and perseverance brought to where she is right now. Her areas of interest include family practice, chronic disease management, aesthetics and preventative medicine. Her holistic approach to patient care includes shared decision making and compassionate quality care. In the future, she wants to precepts students and join medical mission outside United States. Michelle is linguistic, she speaks fluent in tagalog, bisaya and pangalatok and enjoys caring with same dialect-speaking patients as their primary language. Michelle loves to travel around the world, road tripping, food discovering and beach-winding outside work.
